About Me

I am a clinical bioinformatics scientist specializing in clinical informatics, machine learning, and big-data analytics. My work focuses on leveraging artificial intelligence and computational methodologies to enhance diagnostic workflows and improve patient outcomes.

With a background in quantitative sciences, biophysics, and computational biology, I have developed expertise in study design, advanced statistical analysis, and bioinformatics. My research experience spans multiple domains, integrating cutting-edge data-driven approaches with biomedical sciences to drive innovation in clinical diagnostics.

Passionate about translating complex data into actionable insights, I work at the intersection of AI, pathology, and genomics to develop next-generation tools for precision medicine. My goal is to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare, advancing the frontiers of personalized diagnostics.
